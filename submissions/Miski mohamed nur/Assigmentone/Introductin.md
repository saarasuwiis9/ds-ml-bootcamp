 ### What is Overfitting?
 ### Overfitting happens when a machine learning model learns the training data too well, including its noise and details, and as a result performs poorly on new, unseen data.

 ### How Can Overfitting Be Prevented?
--Use More Training Data:More data helps the model learn general patterns
--Simplify the Model:Use fewer features or a less complex model
--Train for Fewer Epochs (Early Stopping):Stop training before the model starts memorizing noise
--Regularization:Techniques like L1 and L2 add penalties for complex models
